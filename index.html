Wellness Program

VEGAN

A shout out to all vegans! We’ve combined a very diverse choice of savory greeneries to help you commit to your healthier vegan lifestyle without feeling the need to try whatever’s on the other side of the grass! The Vegan meal plan is specially designed by our dietitians to prevent any nutritional deficiencies

NUTRI PREGZ

NutriPregz is a personalized plan according to your pre-pregnancy weight and how many kilos you should gain gradually during your pregnancy journey. Our plan provides all the nutrients, vitamins, and minerals essential for you and your baby. And it helps you to get in shape faster after your delivery!

WEIGHT LOSS

Lose fat effortlessly with a guided healthy meal plan that delivers the necessary vitamins & minerals to your body while you burn all the unwanted fat! The weight loss plan is personalized and calorie counted according to your Basal metabolic rate and your lifestyle while giving your body the nutrients it needs!
